# MustafaMoves — Image Guide

All images are stored locally in `/public/images/`. Add your images there and they'll work automatically.

## Folder Structure

```
public/images/
├── hero/
│   ├── gym.jpg          # Hero background (workout scene)
│   ├── preview.jpg      # Preview/AI coach section
│   ├── cta.jpg          # CTA section background
│   └── about.jpg        # About section
├── exercises/
│   ├── bench-press.jpg
│   ├── squat.jpg
│   ├── deadlift.jpg
│   ├── overhead-press.jpg
│   ├── pull-ups.jpg
│   ├── dumbbell-row.jpg
│   ├── lunges.jpg
│   ├── plank.jpg
│   ├── bicep-curls.jpg
│   ├── lat-pulldown.jpg
│   ├── leg-press.jpg
│   └── tricep-dips.jpg
├── guide/
│   └── hero.jpg          # Exercise guide hero
├── auth/
│   ├── login.jpg         # Login page background
│   └── signup.jpg        # Signup page background
└── robot/
    └── mstfa-avatar.svg  # Mstfa AI robot avatar (optional — uses emoji by default)
```

## How to add images

1. Download your images (Unsplash, your own photos, etc.)
2. Place them in the correct folder above (e.g. `public/images/hero/gym.jpg`)
3. That's it — they'll show up on the site automatically

## Recommended image sizes

| Image | Size | Format |
|-------|------|--------|
| Hero backgrounds | 1920×1080 | WebP or JPG |
| Exercise cards | 800×600 | WebP or JPG |
| Auth backgrounds | 1200×1600 | WebP or JPG |
| Preview section | 1200×675 | WebP or JPG |
